# 🏆 Winner of the LIAO Hackathon - Academic League of AI and Optimization at UFBA

I'm very happy to share that last week my team, **Hexagon**, won **1st place** in the hackathon promoted by LIAO at the Federal University of Bahia (UFBA).

The challenge was to create solutions that combined **Artificial Intelligence**, **Smart Cities**, and the fight against **Climate Change**.

This is how **BioPoints** emerged, an innovative system that creates a network of incentives for the consumption of sustainable fuels.

---

## My Role in the Project

Being responsible for the processing, training, and prediction of the dataset, my mission during the two days of the event was to transform raw numbers into the true engine of our product.

It was an intense process, in which I applied:

### 🔹 Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)

I explored the dataset in search of patterns that validated our business rule.

### 🔹 Feature Engineering

I evaluated and statistically verified the weight of critical variables, such as engine characteristics and fuel consumption.

### 🔹 Predictive Modeling

I tested different approaches until focusing on the **Random Forest** model, ensuring error minimization and an accurate **R²**.

### 🔹 Real-Time Integration

I natively connected the results of predictions made in Python with the application's backend architecture.
